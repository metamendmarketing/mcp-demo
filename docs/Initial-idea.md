🔥 Core Concept (Refined)

“AI Buying Assistant for Hot Tub Customers”

An AI-powered tool that:

Educates buyers before they walk into a dealer

Matches them with the right product

Routes them to the nearest distributor

Equips them with confidence + intent

👉 Outcome:
Better leads, faster sales cycles, higher close rates for dealers.

🧠 The Real Problem You’re Solving

Right now:

Customers are confused (jets, pumps, insulation, seating, pricing tiers)

Dealers waste time educating low-intent buyers

Brand has no control over pre-sale experience

Comparison across models is painful

So customers:

Bounce

Delay decisions

Or choose competitors

💡 The AI Tool Vision (Break It Into 3 Layers)
1. 🧭 Guided Buying Wizard (Front-End Experience)

This is the hook.

Instead of browsing specs, users answer questions like:

How many people will use it?

Indoor or outdoor?

Climate?

Budget range?

Therapy vs relaxation vs social?

👉 AI translates answers into:

Needs profile

Feature priorities

Recommended models

2. 🤖 AI Education Engine (Differentiator)

This is where it gets powerful.

Instead of static content:

“What does 40 jets vs 60 jets mean?”

“Is saltwater worth it?”

“What’s energy cost in Canada?”

AI answers dynamically:

Based on THEIR situation

In plain language

With brand-biased (but helpful) framing

👉 Think: ChatGPT trained specifically on:

Their product catalog

Their differentiators

Competitor positioning

3. 🎯 Dealer Conversion Layer (Business Value)

This is what makes it sell.

After recommendations:

Show top 2–3 models

Generate a “Buyer Summary” PDF

Include:

Recommended models

Why they fit

Key features to look for

Questions to ask

Then:

“Find a dealer near you”

Pass lead + summary to distributor

👉 Dealers LOVE this because:

Customer walks in educated

Conversation starts mid-funnel
