WEATHERSHIELD™ HOT TUB COVERS

Premium fabric Weathershield spa covers are 25% lighter than traditional vinyl covers and much easier to maneuver. The custom-fit design reduces heat loss and evaporation, saving money on utility bills. It protects your hot tub’s acrylic surface from abrasion, sun damage, debris and wild critters, and complies with Marquis’ spa-shell warranty.1

Do you live in a windy area, or have young children or small pets? Weathershield covers are UL-approved safety covers, equipped with top-quality safety locks to keep the cover in place and secure. Each cover includes an embedded C-channel support to resist drooping or buckling, especially helpful in areas prone to heavy rain or snow. One Weathershield cover is included with each new Crown Collection hot tub purchase.

WEATHERSHIELD STANDARD FEATURES

  • Form-fitting insulated skirt
  • Full-length linear heat seal along the fold
  • High-density foam insulation
  • Polyester fabric is highly resistant to UV, mildew and mold, and is made from recyclable materials
  • Tapered profile allows rain and standing water to drain quickly
  • Embedded galvanized-steel C-channel support to resist drooping or buckling from snow or ice
  • Tie-down straps, key safety locks, and triple-reinforced fasteners to prevent unwanted cover removal
  • Tested and certified to meet or exceed ASTM F1346-91 safety specifications, and carrying an ASTM Certificate of Product Compliance by Smith-Emery Laboratories and UL-approved
  • One year warranty from date of purchase

1Use of generic or third-party aftermarket covers will void the Marquis spa-shell warranty.

REPLACEMENT HOT TUB COVERS

If you own a Marquis hot tub model year 1997 or newer, a certified replacement cover is available for purchase through your local Marquis Dealer.2 If you do not know the model year or model name, make note of your hot tub’s serial number before contacting your dealer to help them determine the correct cover size. The serial number was included in the paperwork shipped with your hot tub, and is printed on a label inside your hot tub’s equipment bay. It may also be stamped on a silver tag affixed to the base (or kick plate) below the equipment bay.

HOT TUB COVER LIFTS

A hot tub cover lift makes it much easier to remove your DuraCover and return it to a closed position. Beneficial for all hot tub owners, but especially helpful for petite adults or those who find it difficult to lift 25 pounds (11 kg) or more. Marquis-approved cover lifts are made from powder-coated aluminum, which is corrosion-resistant even when scratched. A variety of mounting styles are available, and some cover lifts feature a shock-assisted lifting mechanism.

CABINET MOUNTED

Lift mounts directly to the hot tub cabinet and into the frame. To ensure fasteners penetrate into the hot tub frame, mounting brackets should typically be located one inch beneath the acrylic lip or near the base of the cabinet.

  • Cover Companion™: Marquis’ most popular cover lift. Stores cover on one side of the hot tub and drops it about 8 to 12 inches (20 to 30 cm).
  • CoverMate III™: A bracket supports the pivot arm and uses a shock assist to easily move the cover up and down, locking it into place. The pivot will not release with the lock in place. Cover is stored on one side of the hot tub and leans slightly inward.

UNDERSTYLE MOUNT

Lift mounts directly into a plate or base instead of mounting to the hot tub cabinet and frame. Avoids marring the hot tub cabinet with screw holes.

  • CoverMate™ Understyle: Mounts to understyle base plate instead of cabinet mounting. Stores cover behind the spa allowing for greater visibility. Great for Vector21 hot tubs.
  • CoverMate Freestyle™: Understyle base helps to counterbalance the lift instead of using the weight of the hot tub. Shock assist makes removing the cover a breeze. Great for round hot tubs and Vector21 models (except for the V94 and V94L).
