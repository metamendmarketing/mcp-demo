HEALING THERAPY

“HIGH-FLOW, NO STING HYDROTHERAPY”

Marquis high-flow hydrotherapy therapy is founded on the principle that a better-designed system equals better therapy. By optimizing water delivery from start to finish – from the pump to the jets – Marquis hot tubs deliver the right amount of water to the right areas of the body to achieve the most therapeutic benefit. It is why Marquis hot tubs have a global reputation for delivering:

  • Deeper muscle penetration without skin discomfort ("high-flow, no sting hydrotherapy")
  • Quieter and more energy-efficient operation
  • Better system performance over the long term

Each Marquis hot tub series delivers a unique combination of high-flow hydrotherapy features. Whatever your therapy needs or budget may be, there is a Marquis hot tub for you. Less pain, better relaxation and a more gratifying ownership experience awaits you. So let’s begin!

PUMPS

At the heart of high-flow therapy is pump configuration. Every Marquis hot tub is engineered to optimize pump performance and longevity, with a variety of configurations available in each hot tub series. This variety is intended to provide flexibility for different power-supply requirements, therapy expectations and household budgets. If you will regularly use your hot tub with four or more people at once, you might consider a multiple-pump system. Increasing the system flow rate helps to maintain the feeling of deep muscle penetration in multiple seat locations. Otherwise, there are just a few basic pump distinctions between each Marquis series:

  • Crown Series: All pumps are dual-speed. One-pump and two-pump models are available, with a total system flow rate ranging from 240 gallons-per-minute (908 LPM) to 480 gallons-per-minute (1817 LPM).
  • Vector21 Series: All pumps are dual-speed, with pump and performance upgrades available on selected models. One-pump and two-pump models are available, with a total system flow rate ranging from 120 gallons-per-minute (454 LPM) to 320 gallons-per-minute (1211 LPM).
  • Elite Series: All pumps are dual-speed, with pump and performance upgrades available on selected models. One-pump and two-pump models are available, with a total system flow rate ranging from 120 gallons-per-minute (454 LPM) to 320 gallons-per-minute (1211 LPM).
  • Celebrity Series: The primary pump is dual-speed, with a performance upgrade available on selected models. One-pump and two-pump models are available, with a total system flow rate ranging from 120 gallons-per-minute (454 LPM) to 320 gallons-per-minute (1211 LPM).

CROWN SERIES

Our flagship Crown Series is designed with advanced therapy, performance, luxury, and extra convenience in mind. Each model ensures a flow rate perfectly-matched to our newest system architecture and jet configuration. To supercharge your hydromassage experience, Crown Series hot tubs offer these advanced therapy features:

  • Hydrokinetic High-Flow Therapy delivers an even deeper hydromassage experience. Similar to V-O-L-T on the Vector21 Series, it minimizes turbulence between the pump(s) and jets. But because it is driven by larger pumps, the sensation of power and intensity is amplified. Even so, it retains our characteristic "high-flow, no sting" hydrotherapy experience. No other hot tub brand on the market even comes close!
  • High Output Therapy (H.O.T.) Zones™: High-Output Therapy™ Zones target specific zones of the body with massive water flow thanks to deep-tissue RHK-40 jet clusters. Each RHK-40 delivers up to 40 gallons-per-minute (151 LPM) per jet! Like the weighty palms of a skilled masseuse, H.O.T. Zones are ideal for tight muscle groups in need of something extra. Dynamic Flow Control valves are located near each H.O.T. Zone seat, so you can easily dial in the perfect level of intensity.
  • Dynamic Flow Control valves are powerfully simple. Turning the lever towards red activates the red H.O.T. Zone jets. Turning it towards blue activates the blue therapy jets. Anywhere in between activates all the jets in your seat. Plus, an intuitive air control dials in the perfect sensation.
  • Regal Hydrokinetic (RHK) jets deliver massive flow volumes in three distinct sensations: Target Touch jets provide a directional water flow targeting precise areas of the body. Pulse Touch jets gently massage underlying tissue with rotating swirls of water. Multi-Touch jets create a broader massage sensation for large muscle areas.
  • Specialized Massage Seats offer flexibility and variety. The Deep-Tissue Massage Seat (with Target Touch jets) delivers a focused stream of water ideal for chronic pain or active lifestyles. The Shiatsu Massage Seat (with rotating Pulse Touch jets) helps you relax and rebalance. Thanks to gentle, persistent massage, the Relaxation Massage Seat (with Multi-Touch jets) is a peaceful location to re-center. And the Swedish Massage Seat combines deep-tissue massage with a variety of sensations for all-in-one therapy.
  • The Regal Whitewater-4™ jet delivers everything from gentle foot massage to an exhilarating eruption of leg and foot therapy. Up to 100 gallons-per-minute (379 LPM) of water flow! Available on all Crown Series hot tubs except the Crown Spirit and 1-pump Wish (but is available on the 2-pump Wish).

VECTOR21 SERIES

Vector21 hot tubs are designed with customization, performance and modern style in mind. Each pump configuration drives a highly-efficient water delivery system, culminating in an array of specialized Jetpods. To optimize this unique system architecture, Vector21 hot tubs offer special features:

  • V-O-L-T™ Therapy: Delivering up to 40% more flow volume than standard designs, the V-O-L-T™ system is specifically engineered to achieve superior high-flow with low pressure. This means we have minimized cross-currents, eddies and swirls of water perpendicular to the direction of flow. This highly-efficient system architecture achieves significant hydrotherapy benefits, and runs noticeably quieter than competing brands. The end result is a feeling of deeper muscle-tissue penetration without skin discomfort.
  • Variable Velocity Valves™ (V3) Throttle Control: Exclusive to Marquis, V3 Throttle Control™ valves are Vector21’s primary flow-control system. Throttle from 0 to 100 percent of the available water flow to individual massage seats for fine-tunable control. Share water flow between multiple seats or send a pump’s entire water flow to only one seat. Between three and five V3 Throttle Control valves are available depending on which hot tub you select, located near the seat or pair of seats it activates. With so much individuality, each member of your crew will feel like they are in the driver’s seat.
  • Jetpods and Specialized Massage Seats: The epitome of versatility, Jetpods are designed to handle the increased flow volume of the V-O-L-T system. Each Jetpod style provides a unique massage sensation, distributed among a variety of massage seats. Conal Jetpods feel subdued and relaxing. Directional Jetpods offer a deeper, more focused sensation. Orbital Jetpods rotate dynamically to create a stroking or kneading sensation. Specialized Massage Seat configurations vary between models and may include: Deep-Tissue Massage, Relaxation Massage, Shiatsu Massage or Swedish Massage. Changing massage configurations is a snap. Simply swap Jetpods from one seat to another, or purchase additional inserts for endless possibilities. Learn more.
  • Whitewater-4™ jet (on selected models): With a distinctive red nozzle and the potential to generate over 100 GPM (379 LPM) of water flow, the colossal Whitewater-4 jet is yet another example of Marquis high-flow therapy innovation. Located in the footwell to enhance the benefits of leg and foot massage, this colossal jet churns the water like a thunderous volcano. Available on the V94L, V94, V84L and V84.

ELITE SERIES

Marquis Elite hot tubs are designed with therapy, performance, and affordability in mind. Depending on the features, options and amenities you prefer, a variety of models and configurations are available. Proudly made by craftspeople in Las Vegas, Nevada using a combination of imported and domestic components, Elite hot tubs benefit from Marquis' fundamental approach to high flow therapy. Features include:

  • Perfectly-balanced water delivery: As in all Marquis hot tubs, the Elite Series features a perfectly-balanced water delivery system. Pumps, plumbing and jet configurations are designed to optimize available performance and ensure a beneficial hydrotherapy experience.
  • Simple and intuitive control: One-pump models feature a control valve to divert the pump’s water flow to one side of the hot tub or the other, or to share the water flow on both sides. Two-pump models eliminate the valve, allowing for each pump to service one side of the hot tub, activated by a simple topside control panel. Either method of flow control is easy to understand and operate.
  • Century II jets: The epitome of relaxation, Century II jets are straightforward and reliable. Standard stainless steel jet faces bring a little extra bling (optional on Celebrity versions). Century II jets feature interchangeable inserts to fine-tune your hydromassage experience, and additional inserts may be purchased for added personalization.
  • Whitewater-4™ jet (on selected models): With a distinctive red nozzle and the potential to generate over 100 GPM (379 LPM) of water flow, the colossal Whitewater-4 jet is yet another example of Marquis high-flow therapy innovation. Located in the footwell to enhance the benefits of leg and foot massage, this colossal jet churns the water like a thunderous volcano. (Available on the Woodstock Elite, Vegas Elite and Hollywood Elite.)
