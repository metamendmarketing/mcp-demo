Marquis hot tub jets

SUPERIOR HYDROMASSAGE THERAPY

By design, every Marquis hot tub is a perfectly-balanced system, where jet sizes, jet counts, jet styles, jet types and jet placements match the pump configuration and water delivery system to achieve the best-feeling hydrotherapy. The end result is a feeling of deep muscle penetration without skin discomfort, and a more relaxing and beneficial hot tub experience.

Crown Series: Regal Hydrokinetic (RHK™) Jets

The epitome of advanced therapy. Driven by the Crown Series' revolutionary system architecture, RHK™ jets provide the ultimate combination of penetrating power and therapeutic flexibility. Jumbo RHK-40 jets are grouped into specialized jet clusters called H.O.T. Zones™ to target your trouble spots in need of something extra. Once you feel the full intensity of a Crown Collection H.O.T. Zone, you will understand why no other hot tub brand compares to Marquis.

  • Regal Whitewater-4™ jet: With a distinctive red nozzle and a flow rate up to 100 GPM (379 LPM), this colossal jet delivers truly dramatic performance. Located near the footwell, it is ideal for exhilarating leg and foot therapy. Available on the Crown Summit, Crown Epic, Crown Euphoria, and Crown Resort.
  • Specialized Massage Seats: RHK jets are preconfigured in Specialized Massage Seats to achieve Deep-tissue Massage, Relaxation Massage, Shiatsu Massage or Swedish Massage. Learn more.

Crown high-flow therapy

Vector21 Series: Jetpods

The epitome of versatility. Fueled by the highly efficient V-O-L-T™ system, high-flow Jetpods provide world-class therapy and custom configuration. Jetpod modules can be swapped from one seat to another to fine-tune your hydromassage, or additional Jetpods can be purchased to further personalize your experience. Get ready to be rocketed to another dimension of pain relief and relaxation.

  • Specialized Massage Seats: Jetpods are preconfigured in Specialized Massage Seats to achieve Deep-tissue Massage, Relaxation Massage, Shiatsu Massage or Swedish Massage. Changing configurations is a snap, and additional Jetpods may be purchased for more customization. Learn more.

Vector21 high-flow therapy

Whitewater-4™ jet

The epitome of power: With a distinctive red nozzle and an average flow rate of 100 GPM (379 LPM), the colossal Whitewater-4 jet is yet another example of Marquis high-flow therapy innovation. Located in the footwell to enhance the benefits of leg and foot massage, this colossal jet churns the water like a thunderous volcano.

  • Vector21 Series: Available on the V94L, V94, V84L and V84
  • Elite Series: Available on the Woodstock Elite, Vegas Elite and Hollywood Elite

View more features

Marquis Elite Century II and Celebrity Century Jets

The epitome of relaxation. Marquis Elite Century II jets have stainless steel jet faces to bring a little extra bling. On Celebrity models, stainless steel jet faces are optional. Century II and Century jets have interchangeable inserts to fine-tune your hydromassage, and additional inserts may be purchased to further personalize your experience.
