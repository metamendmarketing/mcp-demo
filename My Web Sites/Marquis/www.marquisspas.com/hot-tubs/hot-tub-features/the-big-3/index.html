The Big 3

MARQUIS' TOP THREE DESIGN FEATURES

WHAT DOES THE "BIG 3” MEAN?

The “Big 3” embodies the trio of groundbreaking design features that make Marquis hot tubs a class above the rest. While every detail is crafted to deliver The Ultimate Hot Tub Experience™, these three pillars are your gateway to understanding the magic of Marquis and why no other brand compares. The “Big 3” includes:

  1. Healing Therapy

  2. Clean Water

  3. Soft Skin

1. HEALING THERAPY

High-flow, No Sting Hydrotherapy

Experience “high-flow, no sting” therapy that soothes muscles and fascia without irritating your skin. Unlike other hot tubs that can leave you feeling itchy or uncomfortable, the advanced high-flow jets provide deep, relaxing relief. Activate the Whitewater-4 jets for thunderous water flows that work wonders on foot and leg pain.

The Crown Series elevates hydrotherapy with Whitewater-4 jets and H.O.T. Zones, delivering targeted high-flow relief to specific muscle groups. The Vector21 Series features Whitewater-4 jets and customizable JetPods, ensuring every jet provides a personalized high-flow massage. The Elite Series enhances relaxation with high-flow therapy and Whitewater-4 jets on select models, while the Celebrity Series offers rejuvenating high-flow therapy designed for everyday comfort.

High-flow therapy features summary

  • Crown Series: Hydrokinetic Laminar Therapy delivers maximum water flow in an entirely new system architecture. High Output Therapy (H.O.T.)™ Zones target specific zones of the body with massive flow volumes. Up to seven seats and 65 jets (including RHK-40 and Regal Whitewater-4 jets), Specialized Massage Seats (Deep-Tissue, Relaxation, Shiatsu and Swedish), and powerfully simple Dynamic Flow Control valves to adjust water flow between the deep-tissue H.O.T. Zones and Specialized Massage jets. Up to two dual-speed pumps and a full suite of luxury amenities and options.
  • Vector21 Series: Vector Optimized Laminar Therapy (V-O-L-T)™ delivers high-flow therapy with up to 40% more flow volume than standard jets, up to seven seats, up to 43 Jetpods (including Directional, Orbital and Conal), one Whitewater-4 jet on selected models, Specialized Massage Seats (Deep-Tissue, Relaxation, Shiatsu and Swedish), up to two dual-speed pumps, enhanced amenities, premium options, fine-tuned V3 Throttle Control valves, and the ability to isolate water flow to specific seats.
  • Elite Series: High-flow therapy with up to seven seats, 50 standard jets, one Whitewater-4 jet on selected models, up to two pumps, additional options, intuitive control at the seat group level, and the ability to isolate water flow to one side of the spa.
  • Celebrity Series: High-flow therapy with up to seven seats, 50 standard jets, up to two pumps, intuitive control at the seat group level, and the ability to isolate water flow to one side of the spa.

2. CLEAN WATER

Constant Clean™ and Constant Clean+™

Constant Clean™ (Multi-Stage Filtration + Ozone): Offering effortless filtration with a multistage process that removes contaminants. With SmartClean™ software, it automatically schedules clean-up cycles after each use and twice daily when not in use. Ozone is 300 times more powerful than chlorine or bromine, reducing chemical use while purifying water safely and efficiently. The corona discharge ozonator with Mazzei injection outperforms UV systems, easing maintenance for hot tub owners.

Constant Clean+™ (Multi-Stage Filtration + Ozone + In-Line Sanitation): ConstantClean+™ offers top-tier water management, blending advanced vortex filtration with dual sanitation, including in-line sanitation and an ozonator. Turning a five-stage process into a nine-stage process. Providing powerful, low-maintenance water care with dual-cartridge in-line sanitation. A mineral cartridge treats water for up to four months, while a sanitizer cartridge gradually releases bromine or SmartChlor® over four to six weeks, ensuring consistently clean and clear water.

 

Availability in each Marquis series

  • Crown Series: ConstantClean+ is standard
  • Vector21 Series: ConstantClean+ is standard
  • Elite Series: ConstantClean filtration is included. ConstantClean+ is standard
  • Celebrity Series: ConstantClean filtration is included. ConstantClean+ is optional (2020 and newer)*

Learn more

*As an alternative to adding both sanitation systems, either the ozonator with Mazzei injection or the in-line sanitation can be added individually.

3. SOFT SKIN

MicroSilk®

Silky, Soft Skin

Transform your hot tub into a rejuvenating skincare experience. Tiny microbubbles deliver oxygen deep into your pores, detoxifying and improving skin health by promoting cell growth, improving circulation, boosting collagen production. This innovative technology helps fight free radicals, kill harmful pathogens, and leaves your skin feeling soft, refreshed, and revitalized.

 Stay Warm, Stay Relaxed

Unlike typical air bubbles that rise and burst, cooling the water and dissolving little oxygen, microbubbles stay suspended in the water and implode, keeping heat energy in. The “sauna effect” improves circulation, accelerates toxin release, boosts immune function, relieves stress, and promotes deeper relaxation—giving you the ultimate rejuvenating experience with every soak.

 Feel better, Sleep Better

Microbubbles release anions at a therapeutic rate, promoting a deep sense of well-being and relaxation. These ions help neutralize free radicals, improve serotonin levels, reduce pain, and promote lasting calm. The result is a deeply soothing, stress-relieving experience that can enhance sleep quality and leave you feeling completely rejuvenated, both physically and mentally refreshed.

 

Availability in each Marquis series

  • Crown Series: Optional on the Summit, Epic, Euphoria, Destiny, Resort, Wish (1-pump version only), and Spirit
  • Vector21 Series: Optional on the V94L, V94, V84L, V84, V77L (1-pump configuration only), and V65L 
  • Elite Series: Optional on the Woodstock Elite, Vegas Elite, Hollywood Elite, Broadway Elite (1-pump version only), and Nashville Elite
  • Celebrity Series: Unavailable
