Monaco Elite

LOUNGE • 5 PERSON HOT TUB • CLASSIC

Due to high demand, this model has limited availability. Please contact your nearest Marquis Dealer for local availability.

The Monaco Elite is a round hot tub that seats five, featuring a full-body lounge, two deep therapy seats, and two bucket seats. Its 120-volt plug-and-play system makes it easy to place almost anywhere. Standard features include stainless steel jet faces, foam-over-plumbing insulation, Twilight™ LED lighting, and ConstantClean+. Optional upgrades include full-foam insulation, Motown™ audio with Bluetooth, and a 240V Performance Upgrade.*

*Optional 240V Performance Upgrade available (USA and Canada only):

This optional upgrade replaces the standard 120 GPM (454 LPM) pump with a higher flow rate 160 GPM (606 LPM) pump. In addition, it upgrades the minimum electrical service requirements to 240 volts and 30 amps, and is also compatible with 50 amp service. Contact your local Marquis Dealer for more information.
