A Look Back at Our Previous Milestone

FUN FACT: For the past 23 years, Marquis has been the preferred provider for Make-A-Wish hot tub wishes. Throughout the year, Marquis and Make-A-Wish partner to help wish kids find relief in the soothing water of a hot tub. With the help of our independent dealer network, we have helped grant almost 1200 wishes across North America for children with critical illnesses and donated over $7.2 million in cash and in-kind discounts.

Support World Wish Day® on April 29th

The hope a wish provides can be transformational, renewing energy and providing children with critical illnesses the strength to fight. It can even have the power to help them overcome their illness. But it doesn’t stop there. A wish can change hundreds of lives. Creating connection. Fostering optimism. And inspiring generosity and kindness.

Each wish makes a unique and lasting impact on every child, volunteer, supporter, and community who experiences it. On World Wish Day we raise our hands. We tell our wish stories and share our experiences. We spread hope and joy. And we invite you to join us.

Learn more at wish.org/worldwishday and follow Make-A-Wish on social media (Facebook, Twitter or Instagram) each week of April for more inspiring stories.
