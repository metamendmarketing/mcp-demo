Elegant and practical

Our approach to hot tub design is both elegant and practical. At Marquis, every decision we make revolves around the question, “How can we improve the user experience?” Whether the answer deals with therapeutic benefits, ergonomic details, ease of use, water care, aesthetics, noise level, installation, durability, reliability or affordability, our goal is the same. Namely, to deliver The Ultimate Hot Tub Experience!™

World-class performance

Whether your budget is small or large, every Marquis hot tub is extraordinary. You may notice that we don’t describe our spas as belonging to different “tiers.” The simple truth is that every Marquis hot tub is world-class, borne from nearly 40 years of meticulous refinement and obsessive attention to detail. Of course, we do offer a variety of choices to meet a broad range of needs, expectations and household budgets. But even our most basic hot tubs are amazing. And if you desire the ultimate in performance, options and features, your hot tub experience only gets better.

Made in the USA of Domestic and Imported parts

All our spas are proudly made by craftspeople in Las Vegas, Nevada using a combination of imported and domestic components. While other spa manufacturers have moved their production out of the USA, Marquis is steadfast on American soil. We continue to achieve success by delivering unsurpassed design, product quality and customer service. When it comes to the relationships we cultivate with independent Marquis Dealers around the globe, we succeed by doing business the right way. Thanks to our outstanding team of employees, Marquis hot tubs feel better, run better, look better, and last longer than more expensive alternatives made by the biggest global brands.

Socially responsible

We take pride in social responsibility. Delivering the ultimate user experience includes the conservation of natural resources, use of recycled materials, efficient use of electricity, and systems optimized to reduce the use of chemicals. Marquis hot tubs comply with the most stringent energy efficiency regulations for appliances by the California Energy Commission, and are fully compliant with European Union Council Directive 2002/96/EC, which includes:

  • Restriction of Hazardous Substances (RoHS) Compliance
  • Waste Electrical and Electronic Equipment (WEEE) Compliance

Nationally: We are honored to be a Corporate Sponsor of Make-A-Wish® in the United States and Canada. Along with our network of generous Marquis Dealers, we have collectively granted more than 900 wishes to children with critical conditions. It is both a privilege to fulfill their wish and to know our spas help to enrich their physical, mental and social well-being. Since the year 2000, our relationship with Make-A-Wish has yielded in-kind and cash donations exceeding $5 million.

Locally: Marquis values local sponsorship opportunities. Our employee-driven Community Involvement Committee focuses on enriching the lives of Oregon children and youth who live or go to school in Marion and Polk Counties. We support a broad spectrum of sponsorships, from athletics, academics, arts and culture, to health and the environment. Sponsorship requests for programs in Marion or Polk County, Oregon can be mailed to: Marquis Corp., ATTN: Community Involvement Committee, 596 Hoffman Road, Independence, OR 97351.

Award-winning legacy

Marquis is consistently recognized for excellence. Every year since 2006, when the TradeCertified Endorsement Program was first introduced, Marquis has been named a Spasearch Certified Brand and consistently recognized as the #1 Brand in Customer Service Responsiveness. Marquis has been named a Consumers Digest Best Buy, and according to the consumer reports website ConsumerAffairs.com, 93 percent of verified customers rate Marquis either 4 or 5 stars (out of 5 stars). We are the first spa manufacturer to receive the top two awards from the National Spa and Pool Institute: the John Holcomb Silver Award for Technology and Innovation and the Eagle Award for Industry Leadership. Among our many local awards, we have been named Oregon’s Large Business of the Year, Manufacturer of the Year and Exporter of the Year.
